Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our membership plans

What's included in the membership plans?

Our membership plans include comprehensive dental exams, x-rays, cleanings, and discounts on most treatments. Specific benefits vary by plan, but all members receive priority scheduling, no insurance hassles, and transparent pricing.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes, our membership plans have no long-term commitment. You can choose not to renew at the end of your membership period without any penalties or cancellation fees.

Do you offer family discounts?

Absolutely! After signing up for an Individual plan, you can add family members at a discounted rate. Our Additional Family plan is $349/year and our Child plan is $299/year.

What if I need emergency care?

Members receive priority scheduling for emergency appointments. We also offer special promotions for emergency visits, and your membership includes discounts on any necessary treatments.

How do I sign up for a membership plan?

You can sign up online through our website or by calling our office directly. Simply click the "Sign Up Now" button on the plan of your choice, and you'll be directed to our registration form. No payment integration is required at this time.
